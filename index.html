Our Science

Most Circadian Rhythm Lights on the Market are Based on Blue Light. Some lighting products feature warm/cool color shifting throughout the day, blue-enriched, or night modes on screens. They work by activating (or not activating by removing blue color) the non-visual melanopsin in your eye sending “action potentials” to your internal clock, but melanopsin is very insensitive to light. This is because it's not a part of your vision forming system. Influencing melanopsin requires unbearably bright light levels to have any significant impact on your internal clock.

TUO’s Circadian Healthy Lighting is New, Different, and Better. The latest scientific research shows that our light sensitive color vision cones can send more “action potentials” to your internal clock than blue-sensitive melanopsin. TUO’s lighting technology uniquely activates these color cones and the circadian pathway to the body clock, resulting in driving large amounts of “action potentials” and shifting your circadian rhythm easily.
